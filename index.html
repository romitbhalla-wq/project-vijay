About Project Vijay

Department of CSBS, in association with CAMP CORP CLUB GGITS, is launching an exciting internal hackathon for students passionate about databases and AI.

Built under the Oracle Academy umbrella, this hackathon – Project Vijay – encourages you to solve a real‑world problem using Applied Database Systems with Oracle AI Database.

Who Should Participate?

This hackathon is open to all students from:

  • Computer Science and all Allied Branches

Whether you’re a beginner exploring databases or an advanced coder comfortable with AI, this is your chance to build something impactful.

Your Hackathon Mission

Each participant will build an individual project that:

  • Solves a clear real‑world problem (e.g., campus systems, local business, public data, health, finance, or education).
  • Uses Oracle AI Database and the capabilities of Applied Database Systems.
  • Shows that you’ve completed the Oracle AI Database course with certificate (submission by 10th April 2026).

Skills You’ll Showcase

Flex your expertise in (but not limited to):

Database Basics Data Modeling Low‑Code Database Apps Data Integration Database Security & Availability Machine Learning Graph & Spatial Data Generative AI (Select AI) Vector Search Data vs Real‑World Use Cases

Combine some or all of these into a project that tells a strong story with clean code, a solid database design, and practical impact.

Winning Criteria

From all participants, the **top 6** students will be selected based on:

  1. Project Quality and how well the problem statement is aligned with real‑world needs.
  2. Tech Depth: how many features and capabilities you’ve integrated effectively (see skills list).
  3. Presentation: clarity, demonstration, and storytelling in your explanation.
  4. Certificate Proof: timely completion of the Applied Database Systems with Oracle AI Database course (by 10th April 2026).

🚀 EPIC Rewards for Top 6

  • 🥇 Oracle Office Tour in Noida – Industry exposure, expert talks, and behind‑the‑scenes insights at Oracle.
  • ☁️ $300 Oracle Cloud Credits – to experiment, build, and extend your skills in the cloud.
  • 🛠️ Oracle APEX Account – access to a powerful low‑code application development platform.
  • E‑Certificates of participation and recognition for shortlisted students.

Call to Action

It’s time to let your creative heads run! Use the next few weeks to:

  • Complete the Oracle AI Database course (deadline: 10th April 2026).
  • Sketch your problem, design your data model, and build your project.
  • Prepare a crisp presentation and demo.

On 18th April 2026, showcase your project and compete for the top spots!
